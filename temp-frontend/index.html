A little bit
about me

I am 20 years old and I am an avid programmer, photographer, and landscape designer.
I enjoy programming things like webservers, bots, image processors, and handy desktop applications!
Most of my code is closed source, but I enjoy creating open source projects from time to time for people to use on their own systems. I also very much enjoy photography! I have professional grade equipment and I love taking portraits, landscapes, and macro shots. Professionally, I am a landscape designer for an engineering firm based in California, where I help landscape architects design plans for parks, communities, and other types of landscapes! Because of this, I am very skilled in AutoCAD, Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, and even various other CAD/CAM programs.

Programming
and what I specialize in

I specialize in backend programming for webservers and web applications! I'm skilled in C#, Javascript, Rust, and I'm learning Lisp to compliment my AutoCAD and CAM skills. I've created web apps and software for companies in order to make their process easier and more efficient, I love helping companies and people streamline their process with software, and I own a company called Qoilo where we create websites for companies and individuals using custom made webservers with a custom CMS built in!

Photography

I enjoy taking photos with my Canon 80D any time I get the chance. I love going out into nature whenever I can to take photos of landscapes, flowers, and animals. If I can't drive out to a nice spot away from the city, I go into the city to see if I can get some nice street shots. I also like taking photos of people and their events. Birthdays, weddings, family photos, They all make for a fun time for great photos that people love! You can view my portfolio here!

Drawing

Professionally, I draw things. I am skilled in drawing technical documents, prototyping products, designing products, places, and spaces. I can also program for CNC machines and other industrial machines using CAD or the GCode the machine interprets. I mostly design parks and communities around California, everywhere from the Bay Area, to southern California. I'm currently using AutoCAD for the most part, but I also use Photoshop and InDesign for our designs, and I plan on learning Sketchup so that I can continue with the designs in a 3D space, creating realistic and aesthetically pleasing renders for our clients.
